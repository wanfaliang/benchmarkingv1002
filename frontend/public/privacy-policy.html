Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Finexus (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites, applications, and services (collectively, the “Service”). It also describes your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Quick Summary

  • We use Google OAuth to authenticate you. With standard scopes we receive your name, email, and profile image (if available).

  • We store only what we need to operate the Service and comply with the law, and we protect it with appropriate security measures.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion at any time by emailing support@finexus.net.

1. Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to users of Finexus and to visitors of our public website hosted under our authorized domains (e.g., finexus.net).

2. Information we collect

Depending on the scopes you approve during sign-in, we may receive:

  • openid, email, profile: basic identity (name, email, profile picture).

  • Log data: IP address, device/browser type, pages viewed, timestamps.

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize the data.
